🧠 How to Play

  1. Press Start Listening to hear a sequence of English words spoken aloud.
  2. Listen carefully — memorize each word and its position in the sequence.
  3. After all words play, a memory countdown begins (3 seconds per level).
  4. Then pick the emoji that matches word #1, then #2, then #3… in the same order.
  5. Words are hidden during the quiz. Use Show Tip if stuck — it costs 5 pts.
  6. Answer without tips for a +5 bonus per word. 100% accuracy earns a speed bonus.
  7. Every level adds more words and shortens the display time. The difficulty scales automatically.
  8. Press 1–4 on keyboard to pick emojis without tapping.
