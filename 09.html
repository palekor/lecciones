StrategyChunk the sequence. Group words into pairs: sun-tree / cat-book. Your brain recalls clusters faster than isolated items.
RecoveryDo not panic after a blank. Recreate the sound, then the image. Recovery improves when you stay in the sequence instead of guessing randomly.
ScoringClean recall wins. Tips and replays are allowed, but they reduce score. Use them to train, not to fake performance.

How to Play

  1. Tap Start Listening.
  2. Listen to the English words in exact order.
  3. Hold the sequence during the countdown.
  4. Choose the emoji for word #1, then #2, then #3.
  5. Use Tip if stuck, or Replay to rehear the full sequence. Both cost points.
  6. Finish the level and review your memory trace.
